TheKnowDough was founded in Amsterdam in 2023 by me, Boyan Mihaylov. It weaves together different paths of research and creation into a shape-shifting body of expertise.


I have always been interested in worldbuilding - the construction of speculative mirrors of our universe, shaped by coherent laws and relationships as the backdrop of intriguing stories.

My journey began in Architecture - where worlds are built in the form of habitable space, reflecting social and existential necessities through the language of material and structure.

I discovered the intersection between design and programming during the early years of the Institute for Computational Design at the University of Stuttgart. Through a close involvement in digitally fabricated medium-scale architectural and design projects, I entered the world of robotic and CNC fabrication and became familiar with mass customisation techniques.

TheKnowDough was founded in Amsterdam in 2023 by me, Boyan Mihaylov. It weaves together multiple paths of research and creation that I have pursued in the preceding decade, becoming a shape-shifting body of expertise.


I have always been interested in worldbuilding - the construction of speculative mirrors of our universe, shaped by consistently imagined laws and relationships as the backdrop of intriguing stories.

My journey began in Architecture - where worlds are built in the form of habitable space, reflecting social and existential necessities through the language of material, structure and tectonics.
